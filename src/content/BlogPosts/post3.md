ENGRAVE has been awarded a new major prize by the Danish Government under the *Into Change 2025* initiative.

The award recognizes our research on the origin of heavy elements, as well as our efforts in demonstrating and promoting core values in research, including curiosity, collaboration, responsibility, respect, and openness.
